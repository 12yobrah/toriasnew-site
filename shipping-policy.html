Shipping Policy

Safe Delivery Across Kenya

At Toria’s Glam Haven, we are committed to delivering your jewelry safely, quickly, and reliably.

📍 Delivery Locations

We currently deliver nationwide across Kenya, including Nairobi and all major towns. No matter where you are, we’ll bring the glamour to your doorstep.

⏱️ Delivery Timeframes

Nairobi 1–2 Business Days
Outside Nairobi 2–4 Business Days

* Please note that delivery timelines may vary slightly depending on your exact location and order volume.

📦 Order Processing

  • Orders are processed within 24 hours after confirmation.
  • You will receive a confirmation message once your order is received.

💳 Payment Options

We offer flexible payment methods for your convenience:

  • M-Pesa: Secure pay before dispatch.
  • Cash on Delivery: Available in selected areas within Nairobi.

📞 Communication

Once your order is dispatched, our logistics team may contact you via phone or WhatsApp to:

  • Confirm your exact delivery details.
  • Coordinate the best drop-off time.

⚠️ Note: Please ensure your phone number is correct and reachable. Delays may occur due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather or courier issues.
